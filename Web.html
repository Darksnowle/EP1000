I personally use Brackets as my main text editor but it's mainly based on self preference.

The reason i prefer using Brackets is because of its convenience. I do not really have to type out the full command as all i have to do is choose from the list of commands shown by th programme.

After we found our preferred text editor, we were told to watch videos on how HTML works. To be specific, the two recommended videos are from Jack Wright. Learn HTML in 12 Minutes and Learn More HTML in 12 Minutes
